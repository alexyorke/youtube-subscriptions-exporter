YouTube Subscriptions Exporter

  1. Go to youtube.com.
  2. Select how you'd like the subscriptions to be exported to your clipboard:
    • As a spreadsheet.
    • As Google Takeout-like/YouTube API-like (beta.)
    • As NewPipe/FreeTube/Invidious (beta.)
  3. Wait until a popup says it was successfully copied.
Note: some thumbnails may show up as broken images in FreeTube because the Invidious backend is rate-limited and can't fetch the thumbnails fast enough.
